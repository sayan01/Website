FBird

Flappy Bird is an iconic mobile game where the player has to tap the screen to flap the bird's wings and keep it airborne all while dodging pipes. This is a minimalist replica of the game using p5.js I made several years ago.

Tap the screen to start, and keep tapping to keep the bird airborne.
